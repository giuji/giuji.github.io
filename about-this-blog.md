## The Web Sucks
Modern web sucks, gone are the days of Web 1.0 and so are its cute personal blogs and humble forums. The Web lost most of its decentralized beauty and is now dominated by all that corporate right-libertarian Silicon Valley bullshit. Every website feels the same, they all share that same sterile feel, probably due to their inhumanily polished interfaces or their stupid little content algorythms. Privacy is a fallacy and censorship and inflationated tool. The explorative magic that browsing the internet repressented is now a long forgotten memory, replaced by passive coonsoming.  
It's time to regain our corner of the internet!
